== Description == 

WpEnhance is a plugin that can bring more authority to your site and increase search engine optimization amongst the millions of Wordpress sites. The plugin also helps you rank better in search engines with pinging power without proxies as your visitors can ping posts and pages. You can also show off your member count, post count, Alexa rank, Inbound links, Facebook likes, Tweeets and much more.

Features include:

* Facebook Sharing of posts and pages.  
* Twitter sharing of posts and pages.
* Visitor Pinging which can drastically increase your SEO.
* Ability to show the bar in the Header or Footer of your site.
* Ability to activate or deactivate features within the bar.
* Ability to change the bar color to match your site.
* Ability to change the text color within the bar.
* Show off your sites Alexa rank to increase Authority. 
* Show off your sites Inbound links to increase Authority. 
* Show off your sites member count to become an Authority.  
* Show off your sites post count to become an Authority. 
* Show off your sites comment count to become an Authority. 
* Show off your sites category count to become an Authority. 
* Show off your sites first post published date. 
* Show off your sites last post published date. 



== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.

e.g.

1. Upload `plugin-name.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory or simply upload the zip via the 'add new' plugin within your Wordpress dashboard.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Your done and can now go to the plugin settings.


== Frequently Asked Questions == 

Q: How do I change the color of the bar?
A: You can change the color of the bar within the plugin settings.

Q: How do I change the color of the text?
A: You can change the color of the text within the plugin settings.

Q: How do I change what shows in the bar?
A: You can change this within the plugin settings.

Q: How does the pinging work?
A: Your visitors can ping your pages within the bar.

Q: Where does the plugin show?
A: The plugin will show at the footer or header of your site depending on the settings you select.

Q: Will this plugin help with SEO?
A: Yes the plugin has built in features to help with search engine optimization such as the ability for your visitors to ping your website and share via Facebook and Twitter.
